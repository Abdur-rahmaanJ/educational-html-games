[Table of Contents](#id-contents)

## Game of Sums ##
[Table of Contents](#id-contents)

Challenge your opponent to a game of sums! Players play in turns.
* The first player chooses a number and presses _Ready_.
* The second player chooses two numbers that add up to the number from the first player and presses _Ready_. If the equation is correct, the second player gets a point.
* The second player chooses a number for the first player and presses _Ready_. Then the first player finds two numbers to complete the equation.
* If there are no numbers available that would complete a sum, the player presses _Pass_.

If one player has no numbers left or if both players pass, the game is finished.
The winner is the player with most points.

If you enter only one name you'll be playing on your own. Try to choose your numbers in a clever way and use up all your cards without having to pass once.
